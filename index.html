Who I Stand For

Dedicated to serving the backbone of our community—those who protect, educate, and serve our great state.

First Responders

I stand for the fireman and the firewoman. I stand for the paramedic and the 911 operator who answers that phone in our darkest hour. They deserve our unwavering support.

Our Veterans

I stand for the veteran who just left the service and is lost, not knowing what to do or where to go next. We must ensure they never feel alone in their transition home.

Educators

I stand for the teachers in overcrowded classrooms struggling to make sure their students understand the curriculum. They shape our future and deserve better resources.
